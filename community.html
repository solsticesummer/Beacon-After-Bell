Community Board

Two shared spaces: a calendar that keeps every deadline in one place, and a wall for posting wins — big ones, small ones, and the ones only you know were hard.

Part 1 — Shared Deadline Calendar

Never miss a date

Scholarship applications, position deadlines, competition sign-ups, and program cut-offs — all in one community calendar.

Crowd-sourced & current

Students add the deadlines they find, so the board stays comprehensive and up to date for everyone.

Part 2 — The Success Wall

Celebrate every win

Post the acceptance, the first competition, the grade you fought for — big or small.

Supportive, not just competitive

A space where people cheer each other on instead of comparing averages.

Posting and a persistent calendar need accounts, so the Community Board is on the roadmap. Tell us what you'd want to see here.
